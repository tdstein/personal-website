About

I work as a software engineer, a data engineer, and sometimes as a data scientist. My interests include distributed systems, databases, real-time-search, analytics, and sometimes statistics.

Recently I left LifeOmic, where I built tools to expand the capabilities of their Precision Health Cloud. I created solutions for search and analytics, information management, and data provenance auditing. Before LifeOmic, I worked at Premier on real-time causal modeling to isolate treatment effects, disparate data alignment, and hospital-level outcome prediction. In 2013 I interned at Rigor, where I built various tools for monitoring and benchmarking website performance.

Hobbies: coffee, music (ambient, electronica, progressive rock/metal), three dogs (Waffles, Pop Tart, and Bagel), rock climbing, skateboarding, percussion, and yoga.
