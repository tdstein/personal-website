About

I'm currently helping battle cancer at Ronin as a Staff Software Engineering on the Data Platform team. We're building out a state of the art MLOps platform, and we're hiring!

My current focus is building high quality software for data scientist. But, I've spent my career wearing a variety of hats; line cook, maintenance technician, academic tutor, marching percussion instructor, data scientist, and software engineer.

Previously, I spent a few years at LifeOmic building tools for the Precision Health Cloud. I created solutions for search and analytics, information management, and data provenance auditing.

Prior to LifeOmic, I worked at Premier, Inc on real-time causal modeling to isolate treatment effects, disparate data alignment, and hospital-level outcome prediction.

In 2013 I interned at Rigor (acquired by Splunk), where I built various tools for monitoring and benchmarking website performance.
